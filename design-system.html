Lexend

Chosen for readability under stress. Replaces Inter, Manrope, Plus Jakarta Sans.

Theme

Dark

Calming for parents in stressful moments. Dark-first, system-aware.

Tone

Calm+Clear

No harsh reds or high-contrast jitters. Soft teal primary on deep forest background.
